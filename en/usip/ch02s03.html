Earthquake and tsunami

Once upon a time Moses crossed the Reed Sea. There is the ebb before tsunami as is well known.

The building standards act is enacted including quake-resistance standards. The federal government prepares the system of earthquake early warning and the tsunami warning system in accordance to the law. The state government builds embankments and tsunami evacuation towers.
