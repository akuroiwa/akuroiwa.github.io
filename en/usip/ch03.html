Repatriation

The federal government restores the lands to Palestinian owners as wide as possible. The government prepares the settlement colony to Palestinian refugees in the event that is impossible. Israeli move to the settlement colony from West Bank areas with the exception of the moderates. Meanwhile, the government must respect the rights of Bedouin.
