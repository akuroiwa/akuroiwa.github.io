Superconducting Maglev

The federal government, neighboring countries and investors establish a international railway company for the SCMaglev (Superconducting Maglev) from Egypt to Turkey after the end of Syrian civil war. Power stations are vital for maintaining this machine. The company also install optical fiber cable side by side with the track. The company receives investment from private banks, Official Development Assistance (ODA) and the World Bank.
